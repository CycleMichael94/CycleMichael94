About

I am currently working toward a Bachelor of Business Associate with a specialization in finance. After narrowing down my interests at Kwantlen Polytechnic, I decided to pursue my education full time at Trinity Western University.

I am a hard working and ambitious employee who invests personally in any organization in which I am placed. I am a natural leader who knows how to create task flows and delegate, but also knows how to work in a team environment.

A person with diverse experiences and an even more diverse portfolio of interests, I draw from many cultures and world views in order to connect with professionals and solve problems effectively and efficiently.

In the future I want to pursue professional designation and work internationally to develop and manage investments for corporations and financial institutions. In the longer term I am interested in bringing my expertise in finance across the world and helping others discover more about finance than what they hear on the news.

Beyond finance, I am passionate about specialty coffee and film photography. While these passions rarely merge with the world of finance I am always looking for intersections between the two. The time, attention and science behind a carefully-made cup of coffee and the deliberateness of film photography are what draw me to these topics. In the same way, attention to detail and curation of financial services and skills to clients is what propels me in that field.
